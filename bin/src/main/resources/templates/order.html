Order instructions

At InhBookWorm.com, customers can easily choose the books they want and place an order online with very simple steps as follows:

1. Select the product to purchase

– Customers choose to view the product they wish to buy. InhBookWorm.com provides detailed information about the book, such as publisher, year of publication, table of contents, to help customers make the best choice. Additionally, InhBookWorm.com allows customers to preview some pages of the book through images.

– After reviewing the product information, customers can click the “BUY NOW” button to purchase the book and proceed to checkout or click “ADD TO CART” if they want to buy more products and continue selecting books.

2. Check the shopping cart

– Customers can click on the “CART” button to view the list and quantity of products they have selected.

– Customers click the “UPDATE” button to update the quantity and total price of the order.

– Customers click the “BUY NOW” button to proceed to the next step.

3. Provide shipping information

– Customers fill in the required information in the fields.

– There is an order note section for special instructions (e.g., delivery time, recipient name).

– Customers are advised to provide clear and detailed information to ensure fast delivery.

4. Choose shipping and payment methods for the order

A. Shipping methods:

Customers choose the shipping method based on their location and the desired delivery time. InhBookWorm.com offers two shipping methods: EXPRESS SHIPPING and STANDARD SHIPPING.

B. Payment methods:

InhBookWorm.com provides two payment methods: Cash on Delivery (COD) and Bank Transfer.

5. Confirm the order

Customers review their “Billing Address”, “Shipping Address”, and “Order Total”. If the information is correct, please click the “SUBMIT ORDER” button to confirm the order.

Note: We rely on the Book Name and/or Book Code to ensure the correct delivery of books.

Thank you for shopping with us!
