<h3 class="text-2xl font-semibold text-red-700 mb-2">From the Dead</h3>

<p>
    Ten years ago, Alan Langford’s burnt body was found in a torched car, and his wife Donna was convicted for orchestrating his murder. Now, after serving a decade behind bars, Donna receives an anonymous envelope containing a photograph that shatters everything she thought she knew — her supposedly dead husband appears to be alive.
</p>

<p class="mt-2">
    With hatred still burning for the man she thought was long gone, Donna is thrown into a whirlwind of questions. Where is Alan? Who sent the photo? And why now, just as she’s about to be released? Determined to uncover the truth, she draws the attention of DI Tom Thorne, whose investigation quickly spirals into a maze of deception and danger.
</p>

<p class="mt-2">
    Thorne is forced far from the familiarity of his London streets and into a disturbing case where truth is buried under layers of lies. As he hunts a man who has faked death and built a new life, Thorne gets closer to a cunning and ruthless killer — one who’s ready to kill again to stay hidden.
</p>

<p class="mt-2 italic text-red-700 font-medium">
    <strong>From the Dead</strong> is a blistering, high-stakes thriller packed with twists, suspense, and emotional intensity. A standout in Mark Billingham’s acclaimed Tom Thorne series, this novel is a haunting exploration of betrayal, vengeance, and the secrets that refuse to stay buried.
</p>
