<section style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; max-width: 800px; margin: 40px auto; line-height: 1.6; color: #333;">
  <h2 style="color: #2c3e50;">Why Choose Phonics?</h2>
  <p>
    The ability to <strong>decode and encode words efficiently</strong> underpins success with reading and writing. 
    Phonics helps children <strong>decode words for reading</strong> and <strong>encode words for spelling</strong>.
  </p>

  <p>
    The <strong>Targeting Phonics</strong> series uses a <strong>systematic synthetic phonics approach</strong> 
    and follows the learning progression outlined in the <strong>Australian Curriculum</strong>.
  </p>

  <h3 style="color: #34495e;">This Book Includes:</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>The 26 letters of the alphabet (<em>graphemes</em>) and their relationship to the 44 sounds (<em>phonemes</em>) of the English language</li>
    <li>The initial sounds of the 21 consonants</li>
    <li>The five short vowels: <strong>a, e, i, o, u</strong></li>
  </ul>

  <h4 style="color: #2c3e50;">Letter and Sound Sequence:</h4>
  <ul style="columns: 2;">
    <li>s, a, t, p</li>
    <li>n, i, d, m</li>
    <li>g, o, c, b</li>
    <li>h, e, r, u</li>
    <li>f, l, j, k</li>
    <li>v, w, y, z</li>
    <li>q, x</li>
  </ul>
</section>
