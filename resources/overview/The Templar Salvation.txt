  <p><strong>📍 Constantinople, 1203:</strong> As the Fourth Crusade unleashes chaos upon the city, a secretive band of Templars stealthily infiltrates the imperial library. Their mission? To recover a cache of forbidden documents before they fall into Venetian hands. With three mysterious chests in tow, they flee—but they’ll never live long enough to know the magnitude of what they’ve uncovered…</p>

A document known only as the <strong>Fondo Templari</strong>: the secret history of the legendary Knights Templar, sealed away for centuries beneath the Vatican.</p>
