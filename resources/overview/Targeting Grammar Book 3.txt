  <h2 style="color: #2c3e50;">Targeting Grammar Activity Book Series</h2>

  <p>
    The <strong>Targeting Grammar Activity Book Series</strong> follows a carefully developed scope and sequence that provides comprehensive instruction in grammar and punctuation, enabling all students to become skilled communicators.
  </p>

  <p>
    Each book delivers basic instruction on grammar conventions appropriate to the year level. The activities guide students through a step-by-step process toward gaining knowledge and understanding of grammar elements and their practical application.
  </p>

  <h3 style="color: #2c3e50;">Areas of Study</h3>
  <p>The books are divided into six sections covering the following topics:</p>
  <ul style="padding-left: 20px;">
    <li>Nouns</li>
    <li>Pronouns</li>
    <li>Adjectives</li>
    <li>Verbs</li>
    <li>Adverbs</li>
    <li>Sentences</li>
  </ul>

  <p>
    Each section includes <strong>definitions</strong>, <strong>explanations</strong>, <strong>examples</strong>, and <strong>activities</strong> designed to help students practise and consolidate their understanding of grammar in both reading and writing.
  </p>

  <p><em>A removable answer section is included for self-assessment and review.</em></p>
</section>
