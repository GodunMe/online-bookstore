<h3 class="text-2xl font-semibold text-green-700 mb-2">Big Love Cooking</h3>

<p>
    From beloved chef <strong>Joey Campanaro</strong>, owner of the iconic <em>Little Owl</em> restaurant in New York City, comes a heartfelt collection of <strong>75 Mediterranean-inspired recipes</strong> rooted in his Italian-American heritage.
</p>

<p class="mt-2">
    <em>Big Love Cooking</em> is all about simple, authentic food served generously — the kind of dishes made to nourish, share, and bring people together around the table.
</p>

<p class="mt-2">
    Inside, you’ll find standout favorites like <strong>Little Owl Crispy Chicken</strong>, <strong>Ricotta Cavatelli with Tomato Broth, Bacon, and Fava Beans</strong>, and indulgent <strong>Brioche French Toast with Stewed Strawberries</strong>. 
    You’ll also discover comfort classics like <strong>Littleneck Clams with Juicy Bread</strong>, <strong>Mom-Mom Pizza</strong>, and <strong>Pork Chop with Parmesan Butter Beans</strong>.
</p>

<p class="mt-2">
    The book is filled with <strong>historical NYC photographs</strong>, <strong>personal stories</strong> from the restaurant, and Joey’s warm, conversational cooking tips that feel like a chat in your own kitchen.
</p>

<p class="mt-2 italic text-green-700 font-medium">
    Perfect for home cooks who crave big flavors, big portions, and the big-hearted spirit of Italian-American cooking. A must-have for fans of comforting, family-style meals made with love.
</p>
