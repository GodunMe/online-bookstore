  <p><strong>Widely celebrated as the ultimate painter of the human soul</strong>, <em>Rembrandt Harmenszoon van Rijn</em> (1606–1669) turned his empathetic gaze inward with unmatched honesty, creating more than 50 self-portraits throughout his lifetime.</p>
