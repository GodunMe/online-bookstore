  <h2 style="color: #2980b9;">Pierre-Auguste Renoir – Painter of Life’s Happiest Moments</h2>
  
  <p><strong>Pierre-Auguste Renoir</strong> placed people at the heart of his art, celebrating human connection, joy, and the everyday pleasures of life.</p>
  
  <p>His vibrant compositions feature dancers, picnics, and Parisians enjoying the world around them—captured with luminous color and the soft brushwork that defines Impressionism. Renoir masterfully created scenes full of warmth and sunlight, evoking the carefree essence of summer and the joy of being alive.</p>
