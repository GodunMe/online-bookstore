  <h2 style="color: #c0392b;">Whispers from the Beyond – A Chilling Tale of Love, Loss, and the Unexplainable</h2>

  <p><strong>What if death isn't the end?</strong> In this spine-tingling novel, a young man defies the boundaries of the living world… and the terrifying truth may shatter everything his mother thought she knew.</p>

  <p>When Alex is told that her son, <strong>Fabian Hightower</strong>, has died in a car accident, she is devastated. But her grief turns to confusion—and then dread—when she swears she saw him alive <em>after</em> the time of his death.</p>

  <p>Trying to dismiss the experience as a trick of the mind, Alex attempts to move on. But her instincts won't let go. Strange occurrences and a deepening sense of dread lead her to a medium—who confirms the unthinkable: <strong>Fabian is trying to come back</strong>.</p>
