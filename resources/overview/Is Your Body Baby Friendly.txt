<h3 class="text-2xl font-semibold text-red-600 mb-2">Understanding Immune-Related Infertility and Miscarriage</h3>

<p>
    This essential guide explores how immune system imbalances can contribute to reproductive failure. It sheds light on how high levels of Natural Killer (NK) cells, genetic incompatibility between partners, and blood clotting disorders can all interfere with a pregnancy’s success.
</p>

<p class="mt-2">
    The book dives into the complex world of <strong>autoimmune conditions</strong>—whether inherited or triggered by environmental factors—and explains how they are a growing cause of infertility and miscarriage today. These immune dysfunctions not only affect pregnancy outcomes, but also act as warning signs for deeper health issues that may affect the mother long-term.
</p>

<p class="mt-2">
    With insightful contributions from leading experts such as <strong>Professor David Clark, Dr. Carolyn Coulam, Dr. William Matzner</strong>, and <strong>Dr. Geoffrey Sher</strong>, this book offers an evidence-based look at one of the most misunderstood causes of fertility challenges.
</p>

<p class="mt-2 font-medium text-red-600">
    Whether you're facing recurrent miscarriage, unexplained infertility, or planning for pregnancy, this book provides the scientific clarity and hope needed to pursue the best path forward.
</p>
